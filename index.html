Services Offered

Schizophrenia

What is Schizophrenia? Schizophrenia is a chronic mental disorder characterized by distortions in thinking, perception, emotions, language, sense of self, and behavior. Common symptoms include hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking.

Treatment Approach: At our clinic, we offer comprehensive care including medication management, psychotherapy, and support for daily functioning. Early diagnosis and treatment are key to improving outcomes.

For personalized consultation, contact us on WhatsApp.

Depression

What is Depression? Depression is a common mental health condition involving persistent feelings of sadness, loss of interest, and impaired functioning. Symptoms may include fatigue, changes in sleep/appetite, and suicidal thoughts.

Treatment Approach: We provide evidence-based treatments like cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), antidepressants, and lifestyle counseling to help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.

For personalized consultation, contact us on WhatsApp.

Sexual Problems Treatment

What are Sexual Problems? Sexual dysfunctions include issues like erectile dysfunction, low libido, premature ejaculation, or pain during intercourse, often linked to stress, medical conditions, or psychological factors.

Treatment Approach: Our clinic offers confidential assessments, counseling, medication (if needed), and therapy to address underlying causes and restore sexual health.

For personalized consultation, contact us on WhatsApp.

Anxiety Disorder

What is Anxiety Disorder? Anxiety disorders involve excessive worry, fear, or nervousness that interferes with daily life, including generalized anxiety, panic attacks, or social anxiety.

Treatment Approach: We use therapies like CBT, relaxation techniques, and medications to help patients manage symptoms and build coping skills.

For personalized consultation, contact us on WhatsApp.

De-addiction (Alcohol, Drugs, Behavioral)

What is De-addiction? Addiction to alcohol, drugs, or behaviors (e.g., gambling) affects physical and mental health, leading to dependency and withdrawal symptoms.

Treatment Approach: Our program includes detoxification, counseling, support groups, and relapse prevention strategies tailored to individual needs.

Stress & Sleep Disorders

What are Stress & Sleep Disorders? Chronic stress can cause insomnia, sleep apnea, or restless sleep, leading to fatigue, irritability, and health issues.

Treatment Approach: We offer stress management techniques, sleep hygiene education, and medications to promote better rest and mental well-being.

Headache Treatment

What are Headaches? Headaches, including migraines and tension headaches, can be debilitating and often linked to stress, dehydration, or underlying conditions.

Treatment Approach: We provide diagnosis, preventive medications, lifestyle advice, and therapies to reduce frequency and severity.

What is Bipolar Disorder? Bipolar disorder involves mood swings between manic highs and depressive lows, affecting energy, behavior, and decision-making.
