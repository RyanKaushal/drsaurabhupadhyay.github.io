About the Doctor

Dr. Saurabh Upadhyay, MS (Psychiatry), is a qualified and dedicated Psychiatrist committed to ethical and patient-centered mental healthcare. With formal training from the Institute of Mental Health and Hospital, Agra, the doctor focuses on understanding each patient’s concerns with sensitivity and professionalism.

The approach emphasizes accurate diagnosis, appropriate medication management, and compassionate guidance to help patients regain emotional balance and improve quality of life.
